Farming Guides

Learn step-by-step techniques, seasonal tips, and best practices for effective farming.

1. Crop Types

  • Cereal Crops: Wheat, Rice, Maize, Barley
  • Pulses: Lentils, Chickpeas, Beans
  • Vegetables: Tomatoes, Onions, Potatoes
  • Fruits: Mangoes, Bananas, Citrus
  • Cash Crops: Cotton, Sugarcane, Coffee

2. Seasons & Planting Time

  • Kharif (Monsoon): June–October – Rice, Maize, Cotton
  • Rabi (Winter): October–March – Wheat, Mustard, Barley
  • Zaid (Summer): March–June – Watermelon, Cucumber

  • Use compost and organic fertilizers
  • Install rainwater harvesting systems

  • Stay updated with agri-tech and local resources
